# Unreal Miner - Funding Proposal

## Executive Summary

Unreal Miner is an AI-powered geospatial platform that transforms satellite imagery into actionable mineral exploration insights. Our MVP demonstrates a complete pipeline from satellite data processing to 3D visualization, positioning us for rapid commercialization in the $12B mineral exploration technology market.

## The Problem

Traditional mineral exploration is:
- **Expensive**: $50-100M per discovery
- **Slow**: 3-7 years from target identification to drilling
- **Inefficient**: 95% of exploration programs fail to find economic deposits
- **Environmentally Impactful**: Extensive ground disturbance

## Our Solution

Unreal Miner leverages AI and satellite data to:
- **Reduce Costs**: 40-60% reduction in exploration expenses
- **Accelerate Discovery**: 50% faster target identification
- **Improve Accuracy**: 75%+ mineral classification accuracy
- **Minimize Impact**: Reduced field footprint through remote analysis
